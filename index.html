Spirited Away

Movie Information

Description

Spirited Away tells the story of Chihiro, a young girl who finds herself trapped in a mysterious and magical world of spirits. To return to the human world, she must work in a bathhouse for the spirits and find her inner strength.

Award Winner: Academy Award for Best Animated Feature (2003)
